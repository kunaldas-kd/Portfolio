Our story

We believe great
software changes lives.

Founded in 2021, Axiom was built by designers and engineers who were tired of choosing between beauty and performance. So we built a platform that refuses to compromise.

"Design is not just what it looks like. Design is how it works — and we're obsessed with both."

We started Axiom because we saw too many teams shipping products that felt like compromises. Beautiful designs hampered by slow code. Fast code that looked like it was built in 2009.

Our mission is simple: give every product team the tools to build things people genuinely love to use — without sacrificing speed, accessibility, or maintainability.
